Nulhus operates like an independent studio for renewable energy projects. We combine engineering rigour with architectural thinking to craft holistic solar and battery solutions that respond to the site, the client and the community.

Our practice is built on collaboration, sustainability and innovation. We believe clean energy systems should be beautiful—economically, environmentally and socially.

What We Do

  • Feasibility & Modelling Techno‑economic analyses that account for grid capacity constraints, as well as scenario modelling for EV and fleet electrification. We quantify generation, storage and financial returns to help you make informed decisions.
  • Design & Delivery Architectural integration of PV and battery systems with metering, controls and interconnection. Our team manages tendering, procurement and acts as owner’s engineer to ensure quality and compliance from concept to commissioning.
  • Optimisation & O&M Ongoing performance monitoring, tariff optimisation and advisory services that maximise returns and adapt to emerging technologies and market conditions.

Environmental Policy

We recognise the tension between affordable clean energy, free trade and secure, well‑paid jobs. Our policy strives to align climate action and social justice so that investment in renewables uplifts communities rather than passing them by.
