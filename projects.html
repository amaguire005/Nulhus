Projects

A selection of our behind‑the‑meter solar PV and battery storage projects. Each project is shaped by its context and community — from industrial fleets to heritage buildings — and demonstrates our holistic approach.

Industrial Fleet Charging

800 kWp solar array and 1.5 MWh battery integrated into an industrial site to support electrification of a delivery fleet. Scenario modelling considered future EV adoption and grid capacity constraints.

City Centre Microgrid

Feasibility and design for a behind‑the‑meter microgrid serving a mixed‑use development with EV charging. Our modelling assessed grid export limits and load‑shifting opportunities.

Heritage Building Retrofit

Sensitive integration of rooftop PV and battery storage into a historic building, balancing architectural conservation with energy independence. Includes O&M optimisation.
