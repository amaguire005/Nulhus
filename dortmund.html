Dortmund

In the heart of Dortmund, we transformed a dense urban commercial complex into a generator of clean energy. The rooftop PV arrays are meticulously arranged to respect setbacks, mechanical equipment and skylights while delivering maximum output.

Our role included grid capacity modelling, integration of battery storage and coordination with local authorities. The result is a self‑sufficient system that cuts operating costs and positions the property as a leader in sustainable development.
